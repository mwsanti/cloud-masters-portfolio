# UCSP 615 - Technology Trends

## Objective:
Explore and analyze current and emerging technology trends through structured research. This general education course focuses on understanding how technological advancements shape industries, societies, and professional landscapes.

## Technologies Used:
* Microsoft Word
* Research Databases

## Deliverables:
* **UCSP 615 Trends Project** - Research paper investigating significant technology trends, their trajectory, and their broader impact on organizations and society.

## Key Concepts Demonstrated:
* **Technology Trend Analysis:** Identifying and evaluating the significance of emerging technologies and their potential for widespread adoption.
* **Research and Communication:** Conducting academic research and presenting findings in a clear, well-structured format.
