# DCL 600T - Cybersecurity Leadership

## Objective:
Develop leadership competencies and professional development skills within the context of the cybersecurity industry. This course emphasizes self-assessment, industry awareness, case study analysis, data-driven decision making, and articulating a professional leadership philosophy.

## Technologies Used:
* Microsoft Word
* Microsoft Excel
* Research Databases

## Deliverables:
* **Project 1: Identify Core Beliefs** - Personal leadership assessment exploring individual values, strengths, and leadership style as they apply to cybersecurity management.
* **Project 2: Trends in the Cybersecurity Industry** - Industry analysis examining current and emerging trends shaping the cybersecurity landscape.
* **Project 3: Legal Zoom Case Study** - Business and cybersecurity case analysis evaluating organizational challenges, risk factors, and strategic recommendations.
* **Project 4: IT Help Desk Data Analysis** - Data-driven analysis of IT help desk operations using Excel to identify patterns, inefficiencies, and actionable insights.
* **Project 5: Final Leadership Paper** - Comprehensive leadership and professional development paper synthesizing course concepts into a cohesive cybersecurity leadership framework.

## Key Concepts Demonstrated:
* **Leadership Self-Assessment:** Identifying core beliefs and personal leadership qualities relevant to cybersecurity roles.
* **Industry Analysis:** Researching and articulating trends that influence cybersecurity strategy and workforce development.
* **Case Study Analysis:** Applying critical thinking to real-world business and cybersecurity scenarios.
* **Data Analysis:** Leveraging spreadsheet tools to interpret operational data and support decision making.
* **Professional Development:** Constructing a leadership philosophy grounded in cybersecurity principles and organizational management.
